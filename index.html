Welcome to HushBox!

Here's how to use HushBox:

  1. Enter a secure passphrase and write your message.
  2. Click "Encrypt" to generate a secure QR code.
  3. Share or download the QR code.
  4. Use "Decrypt" to read messages from a QR code.
